Water Node

Delivering Remote Sensing Data for US Inland Waters

View Learn Explore

The water node website is a collaborative effort between the National Water Center, CIROH, and CoastWatch. The purpose of the website is to intake and disseminate remote sensing data relevant to hydrological modeling, prediction, and analysis. The data comes from a variety of sources from within NOAA, CIROH, as well as potentially other agencies and companies. This data will be disseminated to CIROH researchers, Water Center operations, and the general public.

This website also serves as an educational platform for the use of satellite imagery. We want to enable stakeholders that aren't experts in the processing and acquisition of remote sensing data to use satellite imagery in their research efforts.
